🍁 2025 Wallpe Tar Spot — Training Report (WITHOUT Fungi)

Target = NEW_SC_Mean (stroma count). SOIL and DAP excluded from inputs. This report is WITHOUT Fungi as a feature. Validation: 5-fold cross-validation (small data).

How accuracy is measured: 5-fold cross-validation splits the 24 grids into 5 groups, tests 5 times (each time holding out a different 20%), and averages — so a single lucky split can't mislead. Grids never appear in both train and test (no data leakage). RMSE/MAE are large because the target is a count (0–1,715), which is normal.
